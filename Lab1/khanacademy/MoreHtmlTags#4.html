Recipe #1 for Chicken Tikka Masala

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 500 grams
Yogurt 1 cup
Tomatoes 3 large
Garlic and Ginger 2 tablespoons
Spices (garam masala, turmeric, chili powder) 2 teaspoons each
Heavy cream 1/2 cup

Steps for preparing:

Step 1: Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Cook the chicken in a pan until golden.

Step 3: In the same pan, saut√© garlic, ginger, and tomatoes until soft.

Step 4: Blend the tomato mixture into a sauce, then add heavy cream.

Step 5: Combine chicken with the sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.

Recipe #2 for Greek Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Cucumber 1 large
Tomatoes 3 medium
Feta cheese 1/2 cup
Olives 1/4 cup
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Lemon juice 1 tablespoon

Steps for preparing:

Step 1: Chop cucumber, tomatoes, and feta cheese.

Step 2: Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.

Step 3: Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then toss gently.

Recipe #3 for Classic Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1 large
Sugar 2 tablespoons
Baking powder 1 teaspoon
Butter 2 tablespoons

Steps for preparing:

Step 1: Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter, then add to dry ingredients.

Step 3: Heat a pan and pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.

Step 4: Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side.

Source: Pancake Recipes
